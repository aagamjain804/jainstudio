Case Study

Global Career Advisers

Building a trusted student consultancy brand through strategic design and consistent social media storytelling.

Scroll

About the Client

Global Career Advisers is an education consultancy that guides students in career planning, admissions, and international opportunities. Their focus is on clarity, trust, and long-term student success.

The Challenge

The brand lacked visual consistency and a strong digital presence. Their previous logo and posts did not reflect professionalism, credibility, or emotional connection with students.

My Approach

I focused on understanding their audience, tone, and service goals. The strategy was built around clean branding, relatable visuals, and educational storytelling.

Design Process

The process included logo redesign, color system creation, typography selection, post templates, and consistent layouts for long-term social media growth.

Social Media Content Strategy

I designed multiple Instagram creatives including: admission updates, career tips, motivational posts, testimonials, and informational reels. The system allows easy expansion for future content.

Logo & Visual Identity

The redesigned logo reflects trust, growth, and guidance. A modern color palette and minimal design language were introduced for long-term brand recognition.

Final Outcome

A strong digital identity supported by structured content, professional branding, and scalable social media templates. The brand now communicates confidence and reliability.

Impact

Increased engagement, improved visual consistency, stronger audience trust, and better brand recall across Instagram and digital platforms.
