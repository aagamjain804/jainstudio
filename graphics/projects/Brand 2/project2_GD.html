Case Study

Vinayak Aluminium

Transforming a traditional manufacturing business into a professional brand through strategic design and social presence.

Scroll

About the Client

Vinayak Aluminium is a regional aluminium products manufacturer supplying doors, windows, fittings and customised solutions. The client approached seeking a brand identity that matched their product quality and market aspirations.

The Challenge

The company had zero social presence and lacked a consistent brand image. Clients evaluating their services found inconsistent visual messaging, outdated logo, and no visual proof of quality craftsmanship.

My Approach

I started with a full brand audit, understanding the manufacturing workflow, product value, and customer expectations. The goal was to turn a functional business into a perceptually premium brand.

Brand Strategy & Brandbook

Designed a complete brandbook including logo variations, color palette, typography, visual language, and usage guidelines to ensure brand consistency across platforms.

Social Media Design System

Developed reusable templates for product posts, highlight reels, customer testimonials, and educational content about aluminium solutions. This laid the foundation for consistent posting and higher engagement.

Impact

The brand went from **zero social presence** to a **polished identity** that reflects product quality, builds trust, and creates recall. This improved visibility, professional perception, and client confidence in a competitive market.
