Case Study

Beyond Vigyapan

Creating a youthful, classy, and premium coffee brand concept through strategic identity and packaging design.

Scroll

About the Client

Beyond Vigyapan is a creative agency led by a young entrepreneur focused on building modern, concept-driven brands. The project involved developing a visual identity for a conceptual coffee brand named “Think Brew”.

The Challenge

The challenge was to transform a rough concept board into a complete brand system. The brand had to feel classy, trendy, and premium while appealing to a young audience.

Concept Development

Working closely with the founder’s vision, I translated the concept board into a coherent visual language, defining the mood, tone, and personality of Think Brew.

Logo & Icon Design

Designed a clean and modern logo and icon system that reflects creativity, youthfulness, and premium coffee culture. The identity works across packaging and digital platforms.

Packaging Design

Created coffee can designs for six different flavours, each with its own color identity and personality. The packaging balances visual appeal, shelf presence, and brand consistency.

Flavour Variations

Developed six unique variants including classic, bold, and experimental blends. Each flavour was supported with distinct typography, color schemes, and graphic elements.

Visual System

Built a flexible visual system that can be expanded into merchandise, cafés, social media, and marketing campaigns in the future.

Final Outcome

A complete concept brand with strong identity, market-ready packaging, and scalable visuals. The project demonstrates end-to-end brand thinking from idea to execution.
